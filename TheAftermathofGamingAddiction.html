Content A

Streaming is the delivery of live or recorded media content
to computers and mobile devices via
the internet and playback in real time. Streaming content
includes podcasts,
webcasts, movies, TV shows, and music videos.

•Any type of multimedia file that has already been recorded and
uploaded to the internet falls
under the definition of regular streaming.

•A type of streaming that occurs in real-time
is called live streaming.
